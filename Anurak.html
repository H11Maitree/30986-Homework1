Projects

  • 2019
    Bio-formulation development
     
    Abstract

    Water lettuce is a major problem in water resources and rapid growth. Biological control using Myrothecium inundatum is specific pathogen and efficient causing leaf blight in nature. This research aims to develop bio-formulation development from M. inundatum for controlling water lettuce. Screening for effective strains of M. inundatum were studied growth of colony, spore number and pathogenicity test. The result indicated that M. inundatum isolate-1 and isolate-4 were selected for bio-formulation development for controlling water lettuce. Next, development of solid formulation for M. inundatum isolate-1 and isolate-4 using wheat flour powder and talcum powder were tested pathogenicity in water lettuce. The result showed that solid formulation for M. inundatum using flour powder and talcum powder could disease severity in water lettuce.
